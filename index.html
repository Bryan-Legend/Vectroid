01 / Premise
The Grid
The Grid — a vast digital frontier divided into six territories, each controlled by a rival program. What was once a unified system has fractured into warring sectors.

You are Vex — a program reactivated in dead memory with no identity and no allegiance. The Grid's six territories are sealed behind the abilities of their rulers.

To survive you must defeat each ruler and claim their power. Each ability unlocks new traversal options, opening paths deeper into the Grid and revealing secrets in previously explored zones.

Your goal: reach the Grid's origin and discover what fractured the system.

02 / Player
Vex
Angular chevron shape with a bright core. White outline. Full twin-stick control — left stick moves, right stick aims and fires. Base health of 5 heart containers expandable to 14 via fragments. Energy powers abilities and replenishes over time. Short invincibility dash on cooldown.

Enemies drop XP. 100 XP per level. Lower-level enemies drop less XP.

Each level grants a point to allocate into: Damage, Attack Rate, Attack Range, Crit Chance, Bullet Speed, Move Speed. Points can be reallocated at any time.

05 / Zones & Bosses
The Six Territories
The Grid is divided into six faction-controlled zones, each with a distinct visual identity, enemy composition, hazard set, and boss encounter. Each zone's ruler guards an ability essential for deeper exploration.
THE DEEP
Cyan Zone
Organic, dark, oceanic. Enemies are fast and evasive. A hunt in murky water. Speed and stealth in darkness.

Gating

Open — no ability required. Dark and dangerous but accessible from the start.

Dominant Enemies

  • Orbiters — Circle at fixed radius
  • Leeches — Fast, drain health on attach
  • Phasers — Phase-dash through player
  • Cloakers — Stalking, invisible until striking

Hazards

  • Current Channels — Push in fixed direction
  • Pressure Zones — Slow movement, darken
  • Laser Grids & Electric Fences

Upgrade Cache

Piercing Rounds — hidden behind destructible wall

Layout

Hub-spiral ~8x9. Entry at center, boss adjacent. Spiral descent through quadrants.

DEPTH
The Serpent — Concentric Rings
Phase 1
Circles arena perimeter. Segments fire inward sonar rings. Head lunges across arena.
Phase 2
Submerges to near-invisible. Only sonar pulses reveal position. Surfaces to strike.
Phase 3
Fragments into three smaller serpents that independently chase while firing sonar rings.
THE VEIL
Blue Zone
Crystalline, dreamlike, deceptive. Enemies confuse and misdirect. A puzzle wrapped in a hall of mirrors.

Gating

Requires Sonar Pulse. Hall of mirrors entrance — true path hidden among decoys.

Dominant Enemies

  • Reflectors — Bounce projectiles back
  • Cloakers — Invisible stalkers
  • Disruptors — Reverse player controls

Hazards

  • Phase Walls — Flicker in/out on timer
  • Mirror Panels — Reflect projectiles
  • Distortion Fields — Scramble visuals

Upgrade Cache

Charge Beam — inside false wall revealed by reflected projectile

Layout

Symmetric maze ~8x8. Mirror-image wings. High connectivity, many loops. Boss at (7,4).

VEIL
The Polyhedron — Shifting Shapes
Phase 1
Creates 4 copies. Only real one takes damage. Copies shatter on one hit. Reshuffles.
Phase 2
Projects holographic walls creating shifting maze. Teleports between gaps.
Phase 3
Projects a copy of the player's ship that mirrors movement and fires back.
THE NULL
Grey Zone
Utilitarian, silent, clinical. Enemies are armored and coordinated. A fortress with no personality. Methodical defense and erasure.

Gating

Requires Phase Shift. Sealed security barriers only bypassed by phasing through.

Dominant Enemies

  • Turrets — Stationary firepower
  • Shield Bearers — Must be flanked
  • Tanks — Slow, heavy, resilient
  • Sentinels & Conductors

Hazards

  • Null Zones — Expanding damage patches
  • EMP Pulses — Disable dash briefly
  • Lockdown Gates — Trap until cleared

Upgrade Cache

Shield Capacitor — behind 3 consecutive lockdown rooms

Layout

Linear fortress ~10x4. Horizontal march. Minimal branching. Grid-aligned.

NULL
The Void — Erasing Geometry
Phase 1
Black rectangle at center erases geometry. Grey pulse waves. Destroy perimeter turrets to expose core.
Phase 2
NULL moves, slowly chasing. Contact is instant death. Turrets reposition between volleys.
Phase 3
Fragments into grid of smaller black squares sweeping in formation. Last one holds the core.
THE FORGE
Red Zone
Industrial, aggressive, alive. Enemies hit hard and soak damage. A gauntlet that respects you for surviving. Overwhelming firepower.

Gating

Requires Overclock. Timed security gates need speed to pass through.

Dominant Enemies

  • Tanks — Slow, heavy, absorb hits
  • Bombers — Drop mines from above
  • Pulsars — Radial shockwaves
  • Splitters & Conductors

Hazards

  • Conveyor Lines — Push in fixed direction
  • Furnace Vents — Periodic fire cones
  • Slag Pools — Slow + damage over time

Upgrade Cache

Rapid Cooldown — sealed room via conveyor through vent off-cycle

Layout

Gauntlet ~9x5. Wide corridor with arena breakouts. Conveyors push forward.

FORGE
The Anvil — Orbiting Hammers
Phase 1
Launches hammers that bounce off walls and return. Slams ground sending shockwaves along conveyors.
Phase 2
Superheats to gold. All four hammers rapid-fire. Conveyors speed up. Mines from above.
Phase 3
Detaches and charges, trailing wall of fire. Hammers orbit independently.
THE MIRE
Purple Zone
Organic, parasitic, suffocating. Enemies drain and restrict. An organism that wants to absorb you. Attrition and area denial.

Gating

Requires Burst Fire. Reinforced creep walls — hardened organic barriers.

Dominant Enemies

  • Whip Tentacles — Arc sweep attacks
  • Leeches — Attach and drain health
  • Weavers — Lay tripwire webs
  • Hives — Endlessly spawn swarmers

Hazards

  • Creep — Spreading health-drain patches
  • Spore Clouds — Obscure + reverse controls
  • Tentacle Walls — Grab and hold player

Upgrade Cache

Magnetic Pull — end of longest dead-end branch

Layout

Branching root system ~8x8. Boss at center (4,4). Many dead ends. No straight lines.

MIRE
The Terrain — The Arena Itself
Phase 1
Tendrils sweep arena floor in patterns. Core exposed between waves. Whip tentacles from walls.
Phase 2
Leeches spawn continuously. Tendrils faster. Hive nodes appear spawning swarmers.
Phase 3
Core detaches to center, surrounded by rotating tentacle shield. Arena continues shrinking.
THE SWARM
Yellow Zone
Chaotic, buzzing, overwhelming. Enemies come in massive numbers. A swarm that pities your loneliness. Volume and numbers.

Gating

Requires Leech Drain. Border zone covered in corrupted terrain draining health rapidly.

Dominant Enemies

  • Swarmers — Fast, one-hit, in waves
  • Hives — Constant swarmer generation
  • Orbiters — Deadly orbit rings
  • Splitters — Multiply on death

Hazards

  • Swarm Tunnels — Flowing swarmer rivers
  • Hive Nodes — Indestructible, periodic spawns
  • Pollen Burst — Blind + attract swarmers

Upgrade Cache

Rear Turret — center of densest hive cluster

Layout

Honeycomb ~7x7. Dense hex-grid network. Maximum connectivity. No dead ends.

SWARM
The Hive Mind — Golden Triangle Cloud
Phase 1
Sends swarmer waves from cloud. Compresses into dense ball and charges. Shoot compressed form.
Phase 2
Splits into two smaller clouds attacking from different directions. Both must be destroyed.
Phase 3
Disperses into storm of individual triangles. Bright core hides among them — periodic flashes reveal it.
06 / Lieutenants
Zone Enforcers
Echo
Herald of DEPTH
A flickering school of tiny triangles moving in unison. Delivers warnings to intruders before DEPTH engages. Speaks more deliberately as it begins to doubt.
Refract
Reflector of VEIL
Created to be VEIL's mirror. Bounces the player's fire back at them. Mini-boss guarding the approach to VEIL. Speaks in flat, direct statements.
Warden
Shield Bearer of NULL
Massive shield bearer patrolling the outer ring. Completely silent. Must be flanked to damage. Once a program with identity, now a lobotomized shell.
Crucible
Weaponsmith of FORGE
A bomber flying predetermined routes, dropping mines and incendiary charges. Built from a program that challenged FORGE and lost — both weapon and monument.
Spore
Propagator of MIRE
A weaver that lays corrupted line segments that spread and branch, choking off escape routes. Remembers being separate but MIRE regrows the connections.
Drone Queen
Coordinator of SWARM
A large orbiter directing swarmers with visible command lines. Destroying the queen causes connected swarmers to scatter. Every queen killed is a mercy.

08 / Hazards
Environmental Threats
Laser Grid
Parallel lines pulse brighter before activating. Toggles on/off. Contact damage + blocks projectiles.
Mine Field
Small diamonds with pulsing glow. Proximity detonation with blast radius. Damages all.
Crusher
Thick parallel walls slide together and retract. Instant kill. Telegraphed by glow.
Electric Fence
Posts connected by jagged flickering lines. Persistent barrier. Disable by destroying posts or switch.
Current Channel
Flowing lines that push player in a fixed direction. Must cross or ride strategically.
Pressure Zone
Movement slows, background darkens. Simulates crushing depth.
Phase Wall
Barriers flicker in/out on timer. Pass through when off, blocked when on.
Mirror Panel
Stationary reflectors bouncing player projectiles. Tactical use around corners.
Distortion Field
Visual display scrambles. Enemies hard to distinguish from background.
Null Zone
Patches of absolute black dealing rapid damage. Expand slowly over time.
EMP Pulse
Periodic area-wide flash disabling dash for seconds.
Lockdown Gate
Doors slam shut when triggered. Trapped until all enemies cleared.
Conveyor Line
Moving lines pushing player and enemies. Variable speed.
Furnace Vent
Periodic damage cone. Telegraphed by brightening before firing.
Slag Pool
Stationary amber areas. Slows movement and deals damage over time.
Creep
Spreading magenta patches draining health. Burned back temporarily by shooting.
Spore Cloud
Drifting particles obscuring vision and reversing controls on contact.
Tentacle Wall
Living barriers grabbing on contact, holding player for 2 seconds.
Swarm Tunnel
Narrow passages with flowing swarmer rivers. Crossing deals rapid damage.
Hive Node
Indestructible terrain. Periodically spawns swarmer waves. Only avoided.
Pollen Burst
Golden particle explosion blinding player and attracting swarmers.
Asteroid Debris
Irregular polygons drifting across the screen. Contact damage. Larger asteroids split into fragments.
09 / Items
Universal Upgrades
Hidden in caches within zones. Each provides a permanent passive enhancement found only once in the Grid.
Shield Capacitor
+1 max health. Found in The Null behind 3 consecutive lockdown rooms.
Rapid Cooldown
Reduces dash cooldown by 25%. Stacks twice. Found in The Forge via conveyor timing.
Magnetic Pull
Pickups attracted from further away. Found in The Mire at the longest dead-end branch.
Piercing Rounds
Projectiles pass through the first enemy hit. Found in The Deep behind a destructible wall.
Rear Turret
Auto-turret fires backward at nearby enemies. Found in The Swarm's densest hive cluster.
Charge Beam
Hold fire to charge a powerful piercing shot. Found in The Veil behind a reflected-projectile wall.
